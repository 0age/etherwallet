EtherWallet

An open source, javascript, client-side tool for generating Ether wallets. It was created because many users were having immense trouble setting up the ethereum client on their computers. We hope that it'll help most of us to accomplish day to day tasks without having a fully running client.

Created by kvhnuke and tayvano.
